BookNest

BookNest - A cozy, inviting hub for cataloguing and sharing books.


How is it working?

BookNest is a web application that allows users to create a personal library of books, socialize with other users, and discover new books. The platform features a user-friendly modular design powered by MORPh.js, a custom render engine, that I made specifically for this project. The application is built with three parts: frontend, backend and database. The frontend is built with MORPh.js, backend relies on Node.js, Express.js and Google Books API, and the database is managed using PostgreSQL.

Features

BookNest offers a range of features, including user authentication, book search, book details, user profiles, bookshelves, and social features like following and messaging. The application is designed to be intuitive and engaging, providing a seamless experience for book lovers to connect and share their passion for reading.

Melancholy of Marshmallow

Melancholy of Marshmallow is an immersive game where players, as otherworldly entities, manipulate a marshmallow vessel to influence a diverse, conflicting world. The gameplay revolves around the struggle between preserving this world or aiding an extraspace creature in erasing it, with the quest for truth guiding the difficult decisions.


Full description

RE:Melancholy is a unique game featuring an array of characters, including yourself, the player. Your role diverges greatly from the others, as you exist outside this world, interacting with it only through a marshmallow avatar granted to you. This unexpected intrusion disrupts the marshmallow's life, inciting various conflicts that impact both its existence and the broader world. The game thrusts you into a recurring, enduring quest to shield this world from an extraspatial entity that seeks to erase its memory. Alternatively, you could join this entity in thwarting the efforts of another being striving to maintain the world's existence. Despite this seeming clarity, the decision is far from straightforward. To make an informed choice, you must uncover the realities of this paradoxical world.

Setting

The game immerses you in a beautifully crafted world where varying visuals merge effortlessly, bringing to life an impressive universe through Vaaston, the local magic, and unique character viewpoints. Starting in a late-medieval fantasy backdrop, the game then evolves, blossoming into a magical-industrial epoch, interspersing steampunk and sci-fi elements into its unfolding narrative, thereby enriching its mystery and depth.

About Vaaston

Vaaston is a potent, transformative, and infinite primordial energy pervading the universe, fueling its eternal recurrence, known as the Great Cycle. This unstable force can unpredictably interact with matter or antimatter in four ways: passing through without effect, pushing off, annihilating the object and itself, or unifying with it. The rarest and most potent is the unification, where Vaaston's infinite potential energy is converted into three times more matter. This unification process culminates in the formation of a Chronosphere, a sphere where time aligns with its own. Here, the growth of the unification point halts, and the fusion of past matter with future Vaaston occasionally gives rise to different matter types and, more rarely, living organisms. Through the infinite iterations of the Great Cycle, various life forms, including humans, elves, dwarfs, and fairies, have emerged, all powered by the cosmic dynamism of Vaaston.
